What does Memorix train?

Memorix game is a good exercise for spatial memory. It is a part of memory involved in recording and tracking information about environment. For example it is required to navigate around a familiar town.

  • Memorize shown red tiles

    You will see open red tiles for a short period of time. Try to relax and memorize their location on the matrix.

  • Open the red tiles

    Once the red tiles are hidden open them one by one.

  • Win

    If you succeed to open all the tiles correctly you will get a new level with one tile more.
